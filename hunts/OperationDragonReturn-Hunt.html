Executive Summary

Operation DragonReturn is a suspected China-nexus espionage campaign — assessed with medium-to-high confidence as overlapping the Silver Fox activity cluster — that impersonates the Government of India's Income Tax Department to target taxpayers, tax professionals, corporate finance teams, and tax-infrastructure operators. Victims are lured to govtop[.]one/incometax and download a ZIP (Common_Offline_Utility_ITR-1_to_4_AY2026-27.zip) posing as the legitimate offline ITR filing utility.

The launcher COU_ITR-1_to_4_AY2026-27.exe side-loads a malicious nvdaHelperRemote.dll, escalates via a runas UAC prompt, pulls a steganographic image (lllyd.jpg → C:\Windows\background.jpg) from a delivery server, extracts a second-stage DLL into the Windows Media Player directory, and installs a masquerading service MixedSvc ("Windows Mixed Reality Service") pointing at Mixed Reality.exe. It then injects into svchost.exe, patches AMSI, and runs dual RAT implants (a DcRAT/.NET implant plus a screen-capture module) over encrypted TLS to separate C2 channels for redundancy.

Defender priority: The highest-fidelity, lowest-FP catch is the MixedSvc service masquerade + nvdaHelperRemote.dll written outside a legitimate NVDA install — these are unique to this chain. Hunt those first (Q1, Q2), then pivot on the DcRAT C2 IPs/domains (Q5, Q6).

Hardening — Tiered & Deployable

Immediate (this week — no compatibility risk)
  • Block the infrastructure (M1031): import the Section 10 CSV into Falcon IOC Management and block the six domains + five IPs at the web proxy and firewall.
  • Enable Microsoft Defender Tamper Protection + confirm AMSI is on (M1040) — directly counters the in-memory AmsiOpenSession patch (T1562.001).
  • Turn on ASR "Block executable content from email client and webmail" (rule BE9BA2D9-53EA-4CDD-8E7B-791B6C0A9DB3) in Block mode (M1042) — kills the ZIP→EXE lure delivery (T1204.002).
Near term (1–4 weeks — pilot first)
  • WDAC / AppLocker DLL rules (M1038): block unsigned DLL loads from user-writable and non-standard media directories to defeat the nvdaHelperRemote.dll side-load (T1574.002). CIS Windows Benchmark §18 (Application Control).
  • Restrict service creation + audit 7045 (M1028): remove local admin from tax/finance users; forward Service Control Manager 7045 to the SIEM (T1543.003).
  • UAC "Always notify" + remove standing admin (M1052): defeats the runas elevation (T1548.002). MS Security Baseline: ConsentPromptBehaviorAdmin=2, EnableLUA=1.
  • Email gateway (M1049): quarantine external mail spoofing the Income Tax Department and inbound ZIP-containing-EXE; enforce DMARC on lookalike sender domains.
Strategic (1–3 months — architectural)
  • Enterprise WDAC in enforced mode (M1038) with a signed allowlist — the durable fix for both side-loading and unsigned service binaries.
  • Additional ASR rules in Block (M1042): block process creations from PSExec/WMI (d1e49aac-8f56-4280-b9ba-993a6d77406c), block credential theft from LSASS (9e6c4e1f-7d60-472f-ba1a-a39ef669e4b2), advanced ransomware protection (c1db55ab-c21a-4637-bb3f-a12568109d35).
  • Network segmentation + egress filtering (M1030) so finance endpoints cannot reach arbitrary TLS destinations directly.
